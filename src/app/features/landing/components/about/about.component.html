Our Mission

We know how confusing it can be to buy a home. That is why we created Re-Connect. Thousands of real estate businesses claim to have answers, but when it comes to accurate affordability, only Re-Connect is proven to help buyers avoid disappointment. Our patent-pending system is the first of its kind in the world and uses a sophisticated proprietary system that takes into account vital property information not just price to analyze affordability. We take every aspect of a buyers finances into account to reduce the chances of disappointment. Our unique process also connects buyers with a custom dream-team of real estate professionals, tailored to suit their specific wants and needs. Re-Connect is a better way to buy real estate that gives consumers a simple, unique method to shop for a home smarter, NOT harder!

Maiden A Rios Gomez

Co-Founder & CFO

After relocating to the U.S. from Colombia in 2013, Maiden swiftly enrolled at Worcester State University to obtain a degree despite the challenges of being in a new foreign country. While studying countless hours a day at the University she also obtained her real estate license and started a part time career in real estate sales. She quickly rose to success and established a real estate company with a friend, REEDU, LLC. In the face of adversity and hardship, she graduated from Worcester State University with high honors while managing a business all at the same time.

After seeing multiple vulnerable citizens lose tens of thousands of dollars due to blind-buying errors caused by lenders, Maiden joined her partner, husband, and all-round support system, Julio, in his vision for creating a system based on true property affordability. Today, Maiden is committed to making sure her clients never fall victim to the dark side of the real estate industry.
